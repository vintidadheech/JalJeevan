Our Mission

"To inspire and empower individuals to understand their unique water footprints, shedding light on the unseen water consumption in daily items, and driving a collective change for a sustainable water future."

Our Plan

Educate: Provide comprehensive information about how everyday items contribute to water consumption.
Innovate: Continuously update our digital technology to provide accurate and user-friendly calculations, ensuring anyone, anywhere can assess their water footprint.
Engage: Collaborate with organizations, communities, and individuals, hosting workshops, webinars, and outreach programs about water conservation.
Act: Pledge a part of our revenue towards water conservation projects globally, ensuring that every footprint calculated also aids in minimizing another in the real world.

Our Vision

To live in a world where every drop is valued. Where understanding one's water footprint is as commonplace as knowing one's name, and where sustainable water usage ensures our blue planet thrives for generations to come.

About Us

Born from a passion for conservation and sustainability, the Water Footprint Calculator team is dedicated to making an impact.

We realized the need to educate people about the hidden water consumption behind the items they use daily. From the coffee you drink to the shirt you wear, we wanted to shed light on the real cost of everyday living.

With backgrounds in environmental science, technology, and community outreach, our team is uniquely positioned to bring this vision to life.

Together, we aim to make sustainable choices easy, accessible, and understandable for everyone.
